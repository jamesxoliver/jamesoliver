# Derivations Source

This directory is the private local source for Derivations built from the parallel Definitions corpus. Nothing here is published automatically. Publication requires a separate, explicit decision.

Khazadum is preserved as the compiled record of James's existing framework and as source material for recovery. This corpus may consult it but does not silently rewrite it. New Definitions and Derivations develop here independently, including when deeper decomposition corrects or supersedes claims preserved in Khazadum.

Definitions are atoms. Derivations are molecules. A Derivation combines stable Definitions in a particular logical or causal configuration and makes visible what follows from their relationship. Its contribution may live in the configuration rather than in any ingredient separately.

Do not create Derivations to fill categories or mechanically enumerate combinations. A real question or observed connection supplies the constraints that select relevant Definitions. A Derivation is ready when every load-bearing term has a stable Definition and every bond among them is demonstrated rather than asserted.

The same Definition must retain the same meaning wherever it is used. If a Derivation requires a term to change meaning, the Definition is unstable, a different term is missing, or the proposed bond is false. Shared appearance is not sufficient evidence of shared causal structure.

Existing papers may be audited by decomposing them into Definitions, identifying unstated or unstable ingredients, and repairing the specific bond that fails.
