james oliver

De Fabrica Naturae

Everything is energy wearing different names.

Hold a stone, and you hold arrested motion. Burn it, and the motion returns. What we call matter is energy at rest in a particular shape, and what we call chemistry, life, thought, and the orders that minds build are that same energy taken up into shapes of greater intricacy. One thing runs through all of it. The names change at every scale, and the names are ours.

This is a single derivation, traced from one source upward through every configuration that follows from it, without a break anywhere along the chain. Each step is a change of costume, and the thing beneath the costume never changes. The account is being built.

The fabric of nature, shown rather than asserted. A work in progress.
